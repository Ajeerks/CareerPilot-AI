# CareerPilot-AI

## AI-Powered Career Intelligence & Interview Preparation Platform

CareerPilot-AI is an advanced AI-driven career guidance platform designed to help students, graduates, and professionals improve their career readiness through intelligent automation, NLP, and modern AI technologies.

The platform combines resume intelligence, AI-powered interview preparation, personalized career recommendations, and conversational AI workflows to create a smart career development ecosystem focused on real-world industry requirements.

CareerPilot-AI aims to bridge the gap between learning and employability by providing users with intelligent insights, skill analysis, and practical career guidance powered by Machine Learning and Large Language Models (LLMs).

---

## Key Features

### AI Resume Analysis & ATS Optimization
- Analyze resume quality and ATS compatibility
- Identify missing technical skills and keywords
- Generate intelligent resume improvement suggestions

### AI Mock Interview System
- Role-specific AI interview simulations
- Dynamic technical and HR interview questions
- AI-generated performance feedback and communication analysis

### Personalized Career Guidance
- Career path recommendations based on skills and interests
- Skill-gap analysis and learning recommendations
- Intelligent career roadmap generation

### Conversational AI Assistant
- Interactive AI-driven career support
- NLP-based question understanding
- Real-time AI assistance workflows

### Intelligent AI Workflows
- Resume parsing and structured data extraction
- Recommendation systems for skills and career growth
- Modular AI architecture for scalable future integration
